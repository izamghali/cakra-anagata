About Our Company

We are CV. Cakra Anagata Gemilang is a company that can provide for all your needs to help grow your company. We are sure of our people's quality and experiences that can make huge benefits for our business partners worldwide. As our motto to be a number one provider, we are highly determined to work with huge professionalism. Hence, we always try our best to satisfy our business partners.

Vision

To become a trusted and professional provider for your business need and optimize the potential of human resources and other resources in various regions in Indonesia so that we can contribute to advancing the people's economy.

Mission

  • Responsive and proactive in developing local and inter-local business
  • Becoming a provider goods of international standards for local and inter-local business
  • Play an active role in boosting the people's economy for the welfare and progress of Indonesian farmers and micro-enterprises.
